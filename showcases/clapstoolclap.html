--- layout: default descriptions-english: - This project was inspired by a children’s toy: the rattlesnake. While playing with this toy the wood plates can be clapped together and apart. This functionality was taken over for the stool. Because of the elastic bands around this furniture the stool can take various shapes. - The seating furniture can be used as single-seater as well as two-seater. When used as single-seater the stool needs 4 corner pieces to keep it in that position. As a double-seater there are two additional ledges needed to keep the chair in its form. - The stool is made of four individual plates that have the shape of a square with a side length of 45 cm. For transport the seating furniture can be folded up to a package that measures 45x45x9,6 cm. descriptions-german: - Inspiriert wurde dieser Hockerentwurf von einem Kinderspielzeug, der ‚Klapperschlange‘.
