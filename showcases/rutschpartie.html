--- layout: default descriptions-english: - In many households it is the small kitchen appliances that take up space on the countertop. This design helps to make them disappear quickly and easily in the tall cabinet. - For this system the floor of a high cupboard is removed and replaced with a board, which can be lowered by a rope along a rail system. The rope runs over deflection rollers inside the cupboard. For this design only the rear half of the cupboard is needed. On the front side other things can be placed, such as glasses, plates, etc. descriptions-german: - In vielen Haushalten sind es die kleinen Küchengeräte die den Platz auf der Arbeitsfläche wegnehmen. Um keine große Kraft aufwenden zu müssen, läuft das Seil im Inneren des Kastens über Umlenkrollen.
