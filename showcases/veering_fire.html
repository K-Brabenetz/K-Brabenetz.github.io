--- layout: default descriptions-english: - My aim of this project was to make a fireplace usable in several ways. At a reception or a celebration, the hearth can be raised, because in this case people are standing and it is more pleasant when the fire is at eye level. The object can be lowered when sitting together with friends. - With the large thread inside the outer casing it is possible to turn the fireplace up and down as required. The object is not mobile because the lower half of the hearth is placed underground – this is the counterpart to the fireplace. It is made of cast iron and has a cylindrical shape with a bowl on top of it. descriptions-german: - Meine Zielsetzung für diese Projekt war es, eine Feuerstelle auf mehrere Arten benutzbar zu machen. Das Objekt ist nicht mobil, da sich die untere Hälfte der Feuerstelle unter der Erde befindet - das „Gegenstück“, in das sich das Gewinde hineindrehen lässt.
