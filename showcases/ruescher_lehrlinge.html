--- layout: default descriptions-english: - This project was developed together with my teammates Elisabeth Martin and Onyinyechi Ogbuebele and it was created for the Rüscher Joinery. - The processes of wood connections and surface treatments play an important role in the vocational school. But unfortunately, they are no longer practiced enough due to financial reasons and lack of time. In order to offer trainees more appreciation for their work, we designed 2 different product approaches that apprentices also can disclose via social media. - The design of the tablet was created to give the trainees the possibility to practise surface treatment. In order not to spend too much time on the construction, it is reduced to the minimum. For the prototype, we chose burned wood because this is a method that is rarely used in joinery and it is also a lot of fun. - The aim of the pendant is to show the handmade wood connection. Normally, products contain more than a single connection, but these are not always good at manufacturing, which could ruin the entire product. In order to prevent throwing away practice pieces, individual connections can be practised and the successful pieces can then be sold or given away. descriptions-german: - Das Projekt, das in Zusammenarbeit mit den Kolleginnen Elisabeth MartinElisabeth Martin> und Onyinyechi Ogbuebele entstanden ist, wurde für die Tischlerei Rüscher entwickelt.
