ABOUT KAROLINE BRABENETZ

In 2018 I finished the degree manual & material culture at the New Design University in St. Poelten with distinction. During these studies I learned a lot a lot about the design process and product development as well as dealing with various materials and techniques for product design. After that I was able to expand my skills in the field of planning at my job as a shop designer.

In my free time I deepen my skills in 3D modelling and game development and am always motivated to deal with new tools and techniques.
